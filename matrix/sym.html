Symmetric Matrix
SYMMETRIC

A symmetric matrix equals its transpose. $$ S = S^T $$ Elementwise: $ S_{ij}=S_{ji}$ Matrix form: $$ S = \begin{bmatrix} S_{11} & S_{12} & \cdots & S_{1n} \\ S_{12}& S_{22} & \cdots & S_{2n} \\ \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ S_{1n} & S_{2n} & \cdots & S_{nn} \end{bmatrix} $$
