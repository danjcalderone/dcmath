Skew-Symmetric Matrix
SKEW-SYMMETRIC

A skew-symmetric matrix equals the negation of its transpose. $$ K = -K^T $$ Elementwise: $ K_{ij}=-K_{ji}$ Matrix form: $$ K = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & K_{12} & \cdots & K_{1n} \\ -K_{12}& 0 & \cdots & K_{2n} \\ \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ -K_{1n} & -K_{2n} & \cdots & 0 \end{bmatrix} $$ Note that the diagonal elements are 0.

The eigenvalues of a skew-symmetric matrix are purely imaginary. Similarly to imaginary numbers skew-symmetric matrices represent infinitesimal rotations. The matrix exponential of a skew-symmetric matrix is a rotations $$ R = e^{Kt} $$
